Who I am

Who am I? I'm a 27 year old tech geek. When I'm not rollerskating (inlines), I'm expanding my deep body of knowledge about all things tech. My latest project is an NFC keylock for my computer (arduino + nfc reader = win). I just read the Alchemist by Paulo Coelho, and it's changed my life. I store my random thoughts in a secret tumblr blog read only by a few select troublemakers.

What's any of that mean for you? It means that if you can think of a website, I can turn it into a responsive mobile friendly reality. In fact, if it has to do with a computer, I can probably figure it out. Sound good? Thought so.
